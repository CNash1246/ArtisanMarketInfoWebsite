Welcome!

Are you ready to embark on a journey of artistic wonderment, indulgence, and giving back to the community? We're thrilled to invite you to Charlotte's first annual Artisan Market, a truly extraordinary event that marries opulence with compassion.

We at Charlotte Artisan Market are proud to introduce you to a realm of artistic splendor like never before. As you wander through the market, you'll encounter a world where craftsmanship knows no bounds. From the exquisite intricacies of handmade jewelry to the timeless allure of artisan ceramics, from bespoke fashion that transcends trends to captivating paintings that stir the soul, this event is a celebration of human creativity at its zenith.

Please visit our Featured Vendors page for more information on participating artisans.

Beyond the aesthetics and luxury, the Charlotte Artisan Market stands for something more profound. Every purchase you make contributes to a cause that's close to our hearts: providing access to the arts for underprivileged children in our community. We believe that art has the power to transform lives, and your participation in this event will help us make that vision a reality.

By attending this event, you are not only supporting local artists but also making a difference in the lives of those less fortunate. Join us in making the arts accessible to every child, regardless of their background. Together, we can inspire creativity, imagination, and a brighter future.
